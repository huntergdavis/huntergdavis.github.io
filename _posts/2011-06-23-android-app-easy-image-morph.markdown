Easy Image Morph is a fast and free application to morph two images together. You can select from 10 different morphing algorithms and adjust the morph weighting per photo. You may also save your morphed image out to a new file.

[![](http://66.147.244.180/~hunterda/content/images/2011/06/screenshot291-180x300.png "screenshot2")](/android-app-easy-image-morph)

You can see more info on the permalink page [here](/android-app-easy-image-morph), or download Easy Image Morph from the Android Market [here](https://market.android.com/details?id=com.hunterdavis.easyimagemorph).
