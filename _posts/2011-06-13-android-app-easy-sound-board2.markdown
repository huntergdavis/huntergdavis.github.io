Easy Sound Board is a fast and free application which allows you to load and play audio files from a virtual sound board.

[![](/content/images/2011/06/screenshot119-180x300.png "screenshot1")](/content/images/2011/06/screenshot119.png)

The sound board retains its settings even after a device reboot.[![](/content/images/2011/06/screenshot221-180x300.png "screenshot2")](/content/images/2011/06/screenshot221.png)

You can download it from the Android Market [here](https://market.android.com/details?id=com.hunterdavis.easysoundboard).

Update 4/2012 This is now open source and hosted on GitHub. Check it out at [my GitHub page](https://github.com/huntergdavis).
